Works with IFAW to rescue animals during disasters like floods and earthquakes.

Dr. Dog

Certified therapy dogs visit hospitals, orphanages, and schools to improve mental and physical well-being.

Humane Education

Raises awareness about animal welfare and combats ignorance and cruelty.

Provides physical and behavioral rehabilitation for animals in distress, preparing them for adoption.
